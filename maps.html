Explore the Forza Horizon 5 Map

The Forza Horizon 5 map is an expansive representation of Mexico, filled with diverse landscapes, seasonal changes, and thrilling racing environments. From towering volcanoes to ancient ruins, each area offers a unique driving experience.

La Gran Caldera

La Gran Caldera, an active volcano, is the highest point on the map, offering stunning views and winding mountain roads. This region is perfect for those who enjoy challenging climbs and sharp turns with breathtaking scenery.

Guanajuato

The vibrant city of Guanajuato features colorful buildings, narrow alleys, and unique underground tunnels. Known for its challenging turns and picturesque backdrops, Guanajuato offers an urban driving experience unlike any other in the game.

Ek' Balam Ruins

The Ek' Balam ruins are a nod to ancient Mayan architecture, set in lush jungle terrain. This area is ideal for off-roaders and adventure seekers who enjoy navigating narrow trails and exploring dense, natural landscapes.

Baja California

Baja California is a sandy, coastal region with wide-open dunes and scenic beaches. This area is designed for high-speed off-road adventures, perfect for showcasing the game's dynamic weather with stunning sunsets and challenging sandstorms.

The Rainforest

The lush rainforest region is filled with greenery, muddy trails, and water crossings, making it a challenging environment, especially during the wet season. With dense foliage and slippery paths, this area offers an intense off-road experience.
