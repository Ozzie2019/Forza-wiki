Forza Horizon 5 Updates

Stay up-to-date with the latest additions, seasonal events, and improvements in Forza Horizon 5.

Winter Season Update

New features and seasonal events just in time for winter!

  • New Cars: The 2024 Ford Mustang GT and the Alpine A110 have been added to the lineup.
  • Holiday-Themed Events: Limited-time holiday events offering exclusive rewards and unique car skins.
  • Ice Racing Mode: A new "Frozen Lake" race mode brings icy tracks with challenging conditions.
Performance and Stability Improvements

Enhancements to gameplay performance, visual quality, and bug fixes.

  • Improved Graphics: Enhanced lighting and reflections for more realistic visuals.
  • Load Time Reduction: Optimized loading speeds, reducing start-up and event transition times.
  • Multiplayer Fixes: Resolved connection issues and improved stability in online sessions.
Festival Playlist Updates

New weekly challenges and rewards in the Festival Playlist.

  • Weekly Rewards: Rare vehicles and vanity items available for completing playlist events.
  • Multiplayer Team Events: Weekly team-based challenges for increased XP and unique rewards.
  • Seasonal PR Stunts: High-speed challenges with increased credit rewards and unlockable cars.
New Accessibility Options

Expanded settings for accessibility, making gameplay more inclusive.

  • Colorblind Modes: Adjusted color settings to support various color vision needs.
  • Text-to-Speech Support: In-game menus and chat support for visually impaired players.
  • Enhanced Difficulty Levels: New difficulty options to customize the driving experience.
